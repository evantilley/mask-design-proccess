Synthesis
Essential Features
Synthesizing information from our research, we were able to focus on several important features an ideal mask would have, including:
Comfort
A Maskquerade mask should be able to be worn for long periods of time without causing the user significant irritation.
Aesthetics
A Maskquerade mask should be fashionable so that indiviudals who are concerned about their appearance will not be self-conscious wearing the mask in public.
Availability
A Maskquerade mask should be readily available to a large percentage of the population.
Accessibility
A Maskquerade mask should be user-friendly to many, including those with disabilities.

Target Questions
We ultimately framed our research as three questions to summarize what we had discovered:
1. How can we design masks that are comfortable but do not compromise on functionality?
2. How can we make masks a more socially admirable item?
3. Can we make the masks more equitably distributable?
