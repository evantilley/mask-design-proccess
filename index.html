Synthesis
Essential Features
Synthesizing information from our research, we were able to focus on several important features an ideal mask would have, including:
Comfort
A Maskquerade mask should be able to be worn for long periods of time without causing the user significant irritation.
Aesthetics
A Maskquerade mask should be fashionable so that indiviudals who are concerned about their appearance will not be self-conscious wearing the mask in public.
Reusability
A Maskquerade mask should cater to those who are worried about their impact on their environment by having a high reusability and/or being produced with biodegradable materials.
Functionality
A Maskquerade mask should have a variety of functions, including options for waterproofing and strap-on styles. It should also not exclude target audiences like those who have auditory impairments.

Target Questions
We ultimately framed our research as three questions to summarize what we had discovered:
1. How can we design masks that are comfortable but do not compromise on functionality?
2. How can we make masks a more socially admirable item?
3. Can we make the masks more equitably distributable?

Moving Forward
After our hypothetical testing, we decided that the three mask ideas that the Maskquerade line of masks will focus on are the waterproof masks, the masks designed for those with glasses, and the masks designed for wallets.

Waterproof Design
This design will allow more overall flexibility for those who encounter wet weather while wearing there mask.
Glasses Design
This design increases the overall accessibility of masks by catering to the needs of a large percentage of the population: those with glasses.
Wallet Design
This design prevents indiviudals from forgetting their masks while on their way out, which can save much time and hassle.

Our overall design process allowed us to create 3 masks which ultimately help increase the overall flexibility and range of mask options and thus may encourage more users to wear masks on a daily basis. We think that our line of masks has potential to increase the fluidity of masks in society and look forward to applying the principles of design learned in this exercise to other endeavors.
